The water is closer

October 23, 2025

I forgot to prioritize the leisure or the less of what I became

In through my becoming, I saw that change is approaching

A false synecdoche, the whole is not what was once shame

It’s name is anew and my heart is encroaching onto the same

I don’t believe there is change

I see that from then is just now in course

A course is fixed

Everything on the plain

The analog is coating, nothing else is eclipsed

So when the time runs out, there is no one else to blame

Land over the water, fall, water is falling onto the surface

Approach from the other side, it’s purpose, no it’s what we deserve

So much to say, the thinkers and theorists can perform

It’s only a superficial gathering, they’re really in the nation adorned

With the jewels of your change, now you’ve become like them

There’s no becoming, you were taken in vain
